Pull Back to the Moving Average

This trade is best used on trending charts in a macro stage 2 downtrend. It takes advantage of short term and predictable pull backs using the moving averages.

  • Direction: Short
  • Type: Trend Continuation
  • Holding Time: Scalp, 3-5 bars

80/20 Wide Range Bar

The 80/20 Wide Range Bar is a short term topping or bottoming pattern, or a short term reversal scalp play.

  • Direction: Long, Short
  • Type: Reversal
  • Holding Time: Short Term Scalps

Pull Back to the 20MA

This is a short term continuation pattern that takes advantage of pauses in a bigger uptrend.

  • Direction: Long
  • Type: Trend Continuation
  • Holding Time: Scalp, 3-5 bars
